FAQ

Common questions.

How do I join the server?

Join our Discord server and head to the #Announcements channel to watch out when the smp realase or look by about for the ip.

Is it really free to play?

Yes — completely free. There's no pay-to-win shop or paid rank that gives you gameplay advantages. Everyone starts equal.

What platforms can I play on?

Any device that runs Minecraft Bedrock Edition: Windows 10/11, Xbox, PlayStation, Nintendo Switch, iOS, and Android. Java Edition is not compatible.

Is the server English only?

English is the main language so everyone can communicate easily. That said, everyone is welcome — just make sure your in-game and Discord communication stays in English in the public channels.

Is it vanilla Bedrock?

The server runs on Bedrock but with Java movement. Any additional plugins or datapacks will be announced in Discord when they're added — we keep it minimal to preserve the classic feel, but we still have some plugins to make the experience better.

What are the rules?

Read the full rules in the Rules tab (top of page) or in the Rules tab in our Discord. Short version: no griefing, no cheating, no harassment.
